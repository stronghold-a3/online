The 3-Bridge Communication System

A non-linear architectural design engineered to "change lanes" automatically when primary infrastructure fails. If one bridge breaks, two others sustain the connection.

1

Bridge 1: Offline-First Cloud App

Primary high-bandwidth channel. Guards operate in "Offline Mode," caching posts locally. Auto-syncs instantly upon connectivity restoration.

Auto-Sync Full Data
2

Bridge 2: SMS Gateway Fallback

Low-bandwidth emergency relay. When cellular data fails, incident reports convert to coded SMS texts sent directly to the Ops Manager.

Zero Data Loss Low Bandwidth
3

Bridge 3: Viber/Radio Mesh

Zero-data tertiary layer. In total network failure scenarios, local mesh networks maintain command chain triangulation.

Radio Mesh Instant Voice
