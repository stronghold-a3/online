1. Terms of Service & Security Contracts

Stronghold A3 provides premier security services governed by transparent, legally sound contracts. By engaging our services, clients benefit from our Zero-Liability Guarantee, meaning we assume full responsibility for labor compliance, shielding you from co-employer risks.

  • Zero-Liability Deployment: All personnel are strictly employed under Stronghold A3, fully compliant with DOLE DO-174. Clients are insulated from labor disputes.
  • Rules of Engagement: Our guards operate under strict, legally defined rules of engagement, prioritizing de-escalation, observation, and reporting over physical confrontation.
  • Service Continuity: We guarantee operational continuity through our 3-Bridge Communication System and Typhoon BCP, ensuring your site is never left unguarded during regional disasters.
  • Contractual Transparency: All pricing, inflation-adjustment clauses, and service level agreements (SLAs) are clearly documented with no hidden fees.

2. Privacy Policy & Data Handling (RA 10173)

As a security agency, we handle sensitive data daily. Stronghold A3 strictly adheres to the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). Our Encrypted Document Vault and digital systems ensure that all information is protected against unauthorized access.

Data Category Handling & Protection Protocol
Visitor Logs & Biometrics Digitally encrypted, accessible only to authorized site admins. Purged after 90 days unless required by law enforcement.
CCTV Footage Stored on secure, localized NVRs with AES-256 encryption. Access restricted to client management and Stronghold A3 Ops.
Incident Reports Transmitted via secure cloud channels. Redacted of personally identifiable information (PII) before archival.
Guard Personnel Data NBI clearances, medical records, and PNP licenses are stored in a restricted Digital Vault, accessible only to HR/Admin.

We never sell, share, or exploit client or personnel data. All data breaches are reported within 72 hours to the National Privacy Commission (NPC) and affected clients.

3. Technology & Surveillance Ethics

As we integrate hybrid security solutions (CCTV, AI monitoring, GPS tracking), we uphold strict ethical guidelines to ensure technology empowers security without infringing on civil liberties.

  • Purpose Limitation: Surveillance tech is deployed strictly for asset protection, access control, and guard accountability. It is never used for unauthorized personal monitoring.
  • Transparency: Clients and visitors are informed of CCTV presence through clear signage. Guard GPS tracking is disclosed and used solely for dispatch optimization and safety.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect visual or location data that is strictly necessary for the security mandate of the specific site.
  • Human Oversight: AI-assisted monitoring (if deployed) always requires human verification by our Ops Center before any incident is escalated or reported to authorities.

4. Accessibility & DRRM Commitment (RA 10121)

Security must be inclusive and resilient. Stronghold A3 aligns with the Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act (RA 10121) and advocates for physical and digital accessibility.

  • DRRM First Responders: All guards undergo mandatory disaster response training. During typhoons or emergencies, our personnel act as facility first responders, assisting in evacuations and securing perimeters.
  • PWD Assistance: Our access control protocols prioritize the safety and dignity of Persons with Disabilities (PWDs), ensuring guards are trained to assist without compromising site security.
  • Digital Accessibility: Our Client Transparency Portal and digital incident reports are designed with high-contrast, screen-reader-friendly interfaces to ensure all stakeholders can access security data.
