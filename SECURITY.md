## Core Security Principles
Stronghold A3 operates under three fundamental principles:
1. **Assess** - Comprehensive risk evaluation before deployment
2. **Adapt** - Dynamic response to evolving security threats
3. **Act** - Decisive, professional execution of security protocols
These principles guide every aspect of our operations, from personnel training to technology deployment.

## Regulatory Compliance
Stronghold A3 maintains **100% compliance** across all regulatory bodies, ensuring zero legal exposure for our clients:
### 🏛️ DOLE DO-174 (Contractor Licensing)
- All security personnel are direct employees of Stronghold A3
- Full compliance with labor standards, wages, and benefits
- Clients are insulated from co-employer liability risks
- Regular audits and documentation maintained in our Encrypted Vault
### 🔫 PNP-SOSIA (Supervisory Office for Security Agencies)
- All guards possess valid PNP security licenses
- Mandatory In-Service Training (MIST) conducted regularly
- Background checks: NBI clearances, medical records, PNP licenses stored in restricted Digital Vault
- 15-minute audit readiness for any PNP inspection
### 🔒 RA 10173 (Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012)
- Strict adherence to data privacy principles
- Encrypted Document Vault for sensitive information
- Access controls: HR/Admin-only access to personnel records
- Client data protection through secure digital systems
### 🌊 RA 10121 (Philippine Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Act)
- All guards undergo mandatory disaster response training
- Guards serve as facility first responders during emergencies
- Regular DRRM drills conducted to maintain readiness
- Coordination with LGU disaster response teams

## Data Privacy & Protection
As a security agency handling sensitive data daily, Stronghold A3 implements rigorous data protection measures:
### Data Classification
| Data Type | Protection Level | Access Control |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------|
| Personnel Records (NBI, Medical, PNP Licenses) | Restricted | HR/Admin Only |
| Client Contracts & Site Assessments | Confidential | Operations Team |
| Surveillance Footage | Highly Restricted | Authorized Personnel Only |
| Incident Reports | Confidential | Management & Client |
### Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All digital documents stored in Encrypted Document Vault
- **Access Logs**: Every data access is logged and auditable
- **Retention Policy**: Data retained only as long as legally required
- **Breach Protocol**: Immediate notification and remediation procedures

## Surveillance Ethics
Stronghold A3 deploys surveillance technology responsibly and ethically:
### Guiding Principles
1. **Purpose Limitation**: Surveillance is deployed strictly for:
   - Asset protection
   - Access control
   - Guard accountability
2. **No Unauthorized Monitoring**: Technology is never used for personal surveillance beyond security scope
3. **Transparency**: Clients are informed of all surveillance capabilities and limitations
4. **Data Handling**: Footage is:
   - Stored securely with encryption
