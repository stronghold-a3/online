Quick Start

New to the system? Start here:

  1. 1 Open the app and tap "Sign In" or "Register"
  2. 2 Choose your role and assigned site(s)
  3. 3 Tap "Access Dashboard" to enter the command center

1. Getting Started

Sign in, register, and choose your role

1
Open the App
Launch the Stronghold A3 Security Management System app on your device. You'll see the welcome screen with two options at the top.
2
Sign In or Register
  • Sign In: If you already have an account, tap "Sign In" and enter your credentials
  • Register: If you're new, tap "Register" to create a new account
3
Choose Your Role
Select your role from the list. Your role determines which screens and features you can access:
  • Guard: Field operations, patrol tracking, incident reporting
  • Supervisor: Oversee guards, review incidents, manage sites
  • Ops Manager: Full operational control, crisis management
  • Administrator: System configuration, user management, compliance
4
Select Your Site(s)
For Guards & Supervisors: Choose the site(s) you're assigned to from the list. This ensures you only see relevant information for your location.
Tip: If you're unsure which role to choose, ask your supervisor or administrator. You can always change your role later in Settings.

2. The Dashboard

Your command center for all operations

1
Access the Dashboard
After signing in, tap the "Access Dashboard" button to enter the main command center. This is your home base for all operations.
2
Navigate Using the Sidebar
The left sidebar contains all available modules. Tap any module to switch to it. The current module title displays at the top of the screen so you always know where you are.
3
Mobile Navigation
On phones and tablets, the sidebar is hidden by default to save screen space. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner to open the sidebar and access all modules.
Pro Tip: The dashboard shows real-time data. Refresh the page or pull down to update information with the latest status from all guards and sites.
Mobile Users: The sidebar automatically closes after you select a module, giving you more screen space to work with.

3. Ops Command Center

Monitor all guards and manage incidents in real-time

Live Guard Monitoring

See every guard's current status at a glance:

  • Active Status: See which guards are on duty, on break, or offline
  • Battery Level: Monitor device battery to ensure guards stay connected
  • Last Sync Time: Know when each guard's device last connected to the server

Incident Review

Review the latest incidents reported by guards:

  • Severity Levels: Incidents are color-coded by severity (Low, Medium, High, Critical)
  • Timestamp: See exactly when each incident was reported
  • Location: View the exact location where the incident occurred

Crisis Broadcast

Send Emergency Broadcast
Available to: Ops Managers, Administrators, and Supervisors

In emergency situations, you can send a crisis broadcast to all guards simultaneously:
  1. 1. Tap the "Broadcast" button in the Ops Command Center
  2. 2. Type your emergency message
  3. 3. Select the severity level
  4. 4. Tap "Send to All Guards"

All guards will receive an immediate alert on their devices, even if the app is in the background.
Important: Use crisis broadcasts only for genuine emergencies. Overuse may cause guards to ignore important alerts.

4. Field Patrol (Offline-First)

Log patrols and file reports even without internet

Offline-First Design: The Field Patrol module works even when you have no internet connection. All data is stored locally and syncs automatically when you're back online.

Scanning Checkpoints

1
Locate QR Code or NFC Tag
Find the QR code or NFC checkpoint tag at your patrol location. These are placed at key points around your assigned site.
2
Scan the Checkpoint
  • QR Code: Tap "Scan QR" and point your camera at the code
  • NFC Tag: Tap "Scan NFC" and hold your phone near the tag

The app will automatically log your patrol with the exact time and location.

Filing Incident Reports

1
Tap "Report Incident"
When you observe an incident, tap the "Report Incident" button in the Field Patrol module.
2
Fill in the Details
Provide the following information:
  • Title: Brief description of the incident
  • Description: Detailed account of what happened
  • Severity: Choose Low, Medium, High, or Critical
  • Photos: Attach photos if available (optional but recommended)
3
Submit the Report
Tap "Submit" to save the incident report. If you're offline, the report is saved locally and will sync automatically when you reconnect to the internet.

SOS Emergency Button

Emergency SOS
The SOS button is located at the bottom-right of the screen at all times.

When to use:
  • You or someone else is in immediate danger
  • There's a fire, medical emergency, or security breach
  • Any life-threatening situation

What happens: Tapping SOS sends an immediate emergency alert to all supervisors and ops managers with your exact location.
Warning: Only use the SOS button for genuine emergencies. False alarms may result in disciplinary action.

5. DTR & Payroll

Automatic time tracking and payroll computation

Daily Time Records (DTR)

Your Daily Time Records are automatically consolidated for you. No more manual timekeeping!

  • Automatic Logging: Your check-in and check-out times are recorded when you scan checkpoints
  • Daily Summary: View your total hours worked each day
  • Historical Records: Access your complete work history for any date range

Automatic Payroll Computation

The system automatically calculates your pay, including:

  • Overtime Pay: Extra hours beyond your regular shift
  • Undertime Deductions: Hours less than your required shift
  • DOLE-Compliant Deductions:
    • • SSS (Social Security System)
    • • PhilHealth
    • • Pag-IBIG
    • • BIR (Bureau of Internal Revenue)
Note: All computations follow DOLE regulations. If you notice any discrepancies, contact your supervisor or the HR department immediately.
Benefits: No more manual computation errors. Your pay is calculated accurately and transparently every pay period.

6. Compliance Vault

All your licenses and permits in one secure place

The Compliance Vault is your digital filing cabinet for all important documents. Everything you need for audits and inspections is stored here.

What's Stored in the Vault?

  • PNP-SOSIA Licenses: Private security agency licenses and guard certifications
  • LGU Permits: Local government unit business permits and clearances
  • Clearances: Barangay clearances, police clearances, and other certifications
  • Guard Documents: Training certificates, medical clearances, and employment records

Expiry Reminders

Stay Audit-Ready
The system automatically tracks expiry dates for all documents and sends you reminders before they expire:
  • 30-Day Warning: Initial reminder when a document is 30 days from expiry
  • 7-Day Warning: Urgent reminder when only 7 days remain
  • Expired Alert: Immediate notification when a document has expired

This ensures you're always audit-ready and never caught with expired documents.
Quick Access: Use the search function in the Compliance Vault to quickly find any document by name, type, or date.
Audit Support: During audits, you can instantly retrieve any document in under 15 minutes, as guaranteed by Stronghold A3.

7. Client Portal

View-only access for clients and automated reporting

The Client Portal provides your clients with a secure, view-only window into their security operations. Clients can monitor their sites without being able to make changes.

What Clients Can See

  • Site Overview: Real-time status of all guards assigned to their site
  • Patrol Reports: View completed patrol logs and checkpoint scans
  • Incident Reports: Review all incidents reported at their location
  • Automated Reports: Receive daily, weekly, and monthly summary reports automatically

Automated Reporting

Hands-Free Reporting
The system automatically generates and sends reports to clients:
  • Daily Reports: Summary of all activities from the past 24 hours
  • Weekly Reports: Comprehensive weekly overview with trends and analytics
  • Monthly Reports: Detailed monthly analysis with recommendations

Reports are delivered via email and are also available for download in the Client Portal.
Security: Clients have view-only access. They cannot modify any data, add or remove guards, or change settings. All changes must be made by Stronghold A3 staff.
Transparency: The Client Portal builds trust by giving clients complete visibility into their security operations without compromising data integrity.

8. Settings & Resources

Customize your experience and access important documents

Settings Menu

Open Settings to manage your account and app preferences:

  • Profile: Update your personal information, contact details, and profile photo
  • Security: Change your password, enable two-factor authentication, and manage login sessions
  • Notifications: Customize which alerts and notifications you receive
  • Updates: Check for app updates and manage automatic updates
  • Integrations: Connect with third-party services (for administrators)

Resources Section

Access official documents and protocols from the Resources section in the sidebar:

  • Policies: Company policies, code of conduct, and operational guidelines
  • Protocols: Standard operating procedures (SOPs) for various scenarios
  • Official Documents: Forms, templates, and official company documents

Theme Toggle

Switch Between Dark and Light Mode
Located in the top bar, the theme toggle lets you switch between:
  • Dark Mode: Easier on the eyes in low-light conditions (default)
  • Light Mode: Better visibility in bright environments

Your theme preference is saved and will be remembered the next time you open the app.
Note: Some settings (like Integrations) are only available to Administrators. If you don't see certain options, they may be restricted based on your role.
