Stress Test Methodology

Internal drills simulating official DOLE/PNP inspections. Non-negotiable metric: retrieve ANY requested document digitally in less than 15 minutes.

Admin staff prepares vault. All critical files scanned and verified for completeness.

Step 2: Random Document Request

Management randomly selects guard/site documents requiring immediate retrieval.

Step 3: 15-Minute Challenge

Timer starts. Admin must produce requested documents within strict timeframe.

Step 4: Gap Identification & Correction

Any missing/expiring docs trigger automated alerts and immediate corrective action.
