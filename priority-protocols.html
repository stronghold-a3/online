CRISIS-PROOF OPERATIONAL WORKFLOW

Priority 6 Crisis Protocols

Six battle-tested protocols to maintain uninterrupted security services during emergencies. When the internet dies, our reporting never stops.

1. Crisis Command Chain

Clear hierarchy for emergency decision-making.

Commander

Operations Manager

Toggles "CRISIS MODE" & alerts clients

Ground Lead

Supervisor / Asst. Supervisor

Dispatches relief & coordinates radio

Site Lead

Head Guard

Physical roll call & site safety

2. 3-Bridge Comms

Redundant communication channels ensure zero data loss.

Bridge 1: Cloud SMS

App logs data locally. Auto-syncs when internet returns.

Bridge 2: SMS Gateway

App converts report to coded SMS when no data.

Bridge 3: Radio / Viber

Total network failure? Use two-way radio.

Zero Data Loss Guarantee

3. Absenteeism

Rapid redeployment when guard calls in sick.

1

REPORT ABSENCE

Supervisor logs absence in Cloud SMS app or via SMS.

2

DEPLOY RELIEF

Ops Manager views "Available Relief Guards" map and dispatches nearest qualified guard.

3

RAPID HANDOVER

Supervisor meets relief guard on-site for rapid handover.

Target: Relief deployed within 2 hours

4. Typhoons

Signal No. 2 raised. Site flooding imminent.

1

SECURE SITE

Guard moves to designated safe zone. Logs "Site Secured" via offline app.

2

DISTRIBUTE RELIEF

Supervisor deploys pre-positioned "Bayanihan" relief packs.

3

MONITOR GPS

Ops Manager tracks guard locations. Manual radio check-in every 2 hours if GPS dark.

Head Guard: Accountable for roll call!

5. Power/Network Outages

Total loss of internet and cellular data.

1

LOG OFFLINE

Guard continues digital tours using offline app. Uses offline template for incidents.

2

SMS/RADIO FALLBACK

If app can't sync, send coded SMS: "STRONGHOLD TOUR COMPLETE SITE A NO ISSUES"

3

MANUAL ENTRY

Ops Manager receives SMS/radio report, manually logs into dashboard, updates client.

Emergency Load SIM active

6. Breach / Unrest

Armed intruders or mob violence approaching.

1

HIT SOS BUTTON

Press Panic in App OR blast Radio: "BREACH SITE A ARMED INTRUDER"

2

WAIT FOR COMMAND

Ops Manager issues "DEFEND" or "EXTRACT" order.

3

FOLLOW R.O.E.

Never engage mob. Prioritize life. Observe & report.

Your Life is the Priority

Critical Success Factors

Emergency Load Rule

Every guard has a pre-loaded backup SIM card (Smart/Globe) used only for crisis communication.

Quarterly Drills

Once per quarter, simulate total internet outage and mock breach. Practice SMS/radio fallbacks.

Client Transparency

During onboarding, show clients this workflow. Say: "If typhoon knocks out internet, here's our backup plan."
