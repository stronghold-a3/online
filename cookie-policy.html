Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to secure your session, optimize performance, and power our interactive maps.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site owners. In the context of Stronghold A3, cookies are critical for maintaining the secure, encrypted sessions required by our Staff Portal and Client Transparency Portal.

2. How We Use Cookies

We categorize the cookies used on our platforms into the following groups:

CategoryPurposeExamples
Strictly Necessary Essential for the core functionality of our platforms. Without these, services like the Staff Portal login (Supabase Auth) and secure document vault access cannot function. Session Tokens, Auth Tokens, CSRF Protection
Performance & Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., the Risk Assessment Tool) by collecting and reporting information anonymously. Page Load Metrics, Error Tracking
Functionality & Mapping Allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your region in Eastern Visayas) and power third-party embeds like our service area maps. OpenStreetMap Embed Cookies, Local Storage Preferences

3. Third-Party Cookies & Security

Because we operate in the security sector, we are highly cautious regarding third-party tracking.

  • No Advertising Trackers: We do not use cookies for targeted advertising, marketing retargeting, or selling data to third-party ad networks.
  • Supabase Auth: Our Staff Portal uses Supabase for secure, role-based authentication. Their cookies are strictly necessary for maintaining your encrypted session and are governed by their enterprise-grade security protocols.
  • OpenStreetMap: Our "Service Areas" section embeds maps from OpenStreetMap, which may set technical cookies required to render the map tiles correctly on your device.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device, and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Important Note for Staff & Clients: If you disable "Strictly Necessary" cookies in your browser settings, you will not be able to log in to the Stronghold A3 Staff Portal, access the Client Transparency Portal, or submit the Security Risk Assessment tool, as these require secure session tokens to function.

To learn more about how to manage cookies in your specific browser, visit allaboutcookies.org.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, regulations (such as updates to RA 10173 or GDPR), or our operational practices. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page indicates when the last revision was made.
