2. THE "APP BUDDY" SHADOWING PROTOCOL Mentor & Deploy Guidelines

A peer-mentorship roadmap designed to ensure seamless field training. By utilizing selected tech-savvy "Tech Champions," we bridge operational learning gaps with mutual trust and support.

Step 01 / Pre-check SELECT

The Champions

Operations Manager selects respected, tech-savvy guards across major client sectors. Issuance of custom "Tech Champion" lanyards/badges occurs to denote local functional authority.

RACI: Ops Manager [A]
Step 02 / Mapping PAIR

The Buddies

Match veteran, non-technical guards with Tech Champions. Golden Law: Maintain a strict ratio maximum of 1 Champion mapped to 5 Buddies to prevent mentor overload.

Rule: Max 1:5 Ratio
Step 03 / On-Site SHADOW

The 3-Shift Rule

Champion stands side-by-side with the buddy at the gate for three full shifts. Direct hand-holding, zero-judgment training, and physical validation on system features.

Protocol: Hand-Holding
Step 04 / Audit GRADUATE

Launch Verification

Buddy takes total control of their device and logs solo. Champion transitions to virtual support, reachable instantly through the mobile app's 'Ask My Buddy' message hub.

Status: Active Deploy

The "Zero-Judgment Zone" psychological Safety Directive

Training must be a completely risk-free space. If a guard struggles to input data, presses the wrong button, or forgets their credentials, the Tech Champion must guide them with absolute patience. We make tech friendly, relational, and supportive—no guard should ever feel shamed or reprimanded for their digital learning curve.
