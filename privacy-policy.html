Privacy Policy

Committed to RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act) compliance. Protecting your data with AES-256 Encrypted Vaults.

1. Introduction & Legal Basis

Stronghold A3 Security Agency ("we", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in full compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), its Implementing Rules and Regulations, and the principles of the GDPR. This policy explains how we handle data collected through our physical security operations and digital platforms.

3. The Encrypted Document Vault & Security

We do not rely on fragile paper files. All sensitive documents—including PNP-SOSIA licenses, NBI clearances, client contracts, and payroll records—are stored in our Digital Encrypted Vault.

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 standards.
  • Access Control: Strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) ensures only authorized Admin/HR personnel can access sensitive guard or client data.
  • Audit Trails: Every access, download, or modification within the Vault is logged and timestamped for DPA compliance.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell your personal data. We only share data with third parties under strict conditions:

  • Government Authorities: PNP, DOLE, LGUs, or the National Privacy Commission (NPC) when legally mandated or during active investigations.
  • Client Management: Incident reports and guard logs are shared exclusively with the authorized management of the client facility.
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase) and SMS gateways, bound by strict Data Processing Agreements (DPAs).

5. Your Rights Under RA 10173

You have the right to be informed, access, object to processing, rectify, and erasure/blocking of your personal data. To exercise these rights, or to report a data breach, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at compliance@strongholda3.com. We will respond within 15 business days.
